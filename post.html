Relevant Experience

Upon entering BYU, I wanted to gain as much experience in the Financial Services industry. My first expereince was at Reef Capital Partners, where I was a Private Credit Summer Analyst. I was able to work in a field which is called Opportunistic Credit

Working in Private Credit led me to my next internship opportunity at Columbia West Capital which is a boutique investment bank. It was here that I gained relevant transaction expereince such as financial modeling and assembling marketing materials.

After working in Investment Banking, I knew I wanted to gain more transactional experience, so I got an internship at a search fund called Ironclad Growth partners. It was here that I gained hands on experience sourcing potential deals and evaluating them based on operational and financial metrics.

Ironclad taught me a great deal about how to communicate with small business owners. This was crucial at my next internship at Brigham Capital, where I was a L/S Analyst. I was able to effectively commuincate with founders and learn more about their comapneis and their respective industries.

Currently I am an intern at Diversis Capital, where I am one of three analysts staffed with assisting the Associates with their due diligence. The tasks may include market research, market mapping, valuation analysis, and document consolidation.

Future Opportunities

The opportunity to intern at various institutions has allowed me to better understand what it is that I want for my long-term career. Due to my internship experience, I have solidified my interest in Investment Banking.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.

Due to this growing interest in Investment Banking, I will be interning come Summer 2026 at Banco Santander in their Financial Sponsors group. I hope to gain more experience in the industry and remain long-term.

The internship with Banco Santander will be in their Los Angeles office located in Century City. I believe this location will offer me the best opportunity to progress in my career, but also remain true to my West Coast roots.
